About LetsClubTogether

Transforming community fundraising through the power of collective purchasing

Our Mission

LetsClubTogether was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make community fundraising sustainable, effortless, and beneficial for everyone involved. We believe that supporting your local sports club, school, or charity shouldn't require constant raffles, bag packing, or door-to-door collections.

By leveraging the collective purchasing power of community members, we create a win-win-win situation where organizations raise funds, members save money, and local businesses gain loyal customers.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, LetsClubTogether emerged from firsthand experience with the challenges of traditional fundraising. Our founders, having spent years organizing fundraisers for local GAA clubs, recognized the need for a modern, sustainable solution.
