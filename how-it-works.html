How LetsClubTogether Works

A simple, transparent system that benefits everyone

1

Organization Registers & Gets Verified

Sports clubs, schools, and community groups sign up on our platform. We verify each organization to ensure legitimacy and protect our members.

  • ✓ Quick online application (less than 10 minutes)
  • ✓ Verification typically completed within 48 hours
  • ✓ No upfront costs or hidden fees
2

Members Join & Link Their Accounts

Organization members download our app and link to their chosen organizations. Members can support multiple organizations simultaneously.

  • ✓ Free mobile app for iOS and Android
  • ✓ Secure account creation with email verification
  • ✓ Choose how to split support between multiple organizations
3

Shop at Partner Suppliers

Members make purchases at our network of partner suppliers. Transactions are automatically tracked - no vouchers or codes needed.

  • ✓ 100+ partner suppliers across Ireland
  • ✓ Automatic tracking via linked payment cards
  • ✓ Exclusive member discounts on top of rebates
4

Automatic Rebate Distribution

Rebates from purchases are automatically calculated and distributed monthly. 70% goes to your chosen organizations, 30% supports the platform.

  • ✓ Transparent rebate calculations
  • ✓ Monthly direct deposits to organizations
  • ✓ Real-time tracking dashboard for all parties

Frequently Asked Questions

How do purchases get tracked?

Members link their payment cards securely to our app. When they make purchases at partner suppliers, transactions are automatically tracked. No receipts or manual submissions required.

When do organizations receive funds?

Rebates are calculated at the end of each month and distributed within 5 business days. Organizations can track pending and received funds in real-time through their dashboard.

Can members support multiple organizations?

Yes! Members can link to multiple organizations and choose how to split their rebates. For example, 50% to their GAA club and 50% to their child's school.

What types of purchases qualify?

Currently focusing on fuel purchases at major petrol stations. We're expanding to include groceries, utilities, and other everyday purchases throughout 2025.
